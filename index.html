About Us

We at KAUTUKUM aim to provide a platform for all to let you discover, learn and teach your hobbies and passion.

  • Live sessions, courses, seminars and meetups with experts to let you judge whether you're made for a particular passion/hobby or not.
  • Trial courses to help you discover a passion for you.
  • Understand the cores and basics of a particular field without actually diving deep into it, making it less time and money consuming for you to discover your true passion.
  • Focussing on all age groups so that everyone can explore and learn irrespective of their age.
